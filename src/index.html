Kayn Arena Build

A bad, low gold player's guide on how to build lethality Kayn in League of Legends' Arena mode.

Kayn's Abilities

What Kayn's abilities do, how you should use your passive ability, leveling order for ability upgrades, and how to use them.

Abilities

Click on an icon to view the ability

Passive

Typically, you'll want to go Kayn's Red form for its survivability as well as its W crowd control. However, if more than half the players in the game are ranged/squishy champions, you can go Blue form.

Ability Level Order

First, max Q, then E, then W leveling up your R whenever you can!

How to Use Abilities

Q is going to be your main damage dealer. You can use your E to both heal and approach the enemy. Once close enough, you can either auto attack or Q, and then W, weaving auto attacks in between abilities. When you are low HP, you can R into the enemy to wait for cooldowns to refresh and to heal.

Kayn's Best Items

The prismatic and legendary items that I like to build every game if I can. In general, I like to prioritize damage and speed, so pick items accordingly

Build Order

I like to start the game with Guardian's Dirk for damage and then buy CD boots for higher DPS. For the first item, Youmuu's gives you a decent amount of damage and speed. For your first prismatic, you need to roll Duskblade or the game becomes almost unplayable. The damage increase on your abilities is really important to get. Then I like to go Serylda's for the armor penetration, and ending with two decent prismatics. If you do not have enough of the second to last prismatic, Edge of Night is always good. For prismatics I like to go, see the next section.

Duskblade of Draktharr

Unique - Nightstalker: Your abilities deal (0% - 22.5%) (based on target's missing health) increased damage against enemy champions. Scoring a takedown against an enemy champion within 3 seconds of damaging them renders you untargetable for 1.5 seconds, which ends prematurely if you perform an action that stealth breaks stealth.

Prowler's Claw

Unique - Sandswipe: Dash to 150 units beyond the target enemy champion's location, and upon completing the dash, deal 12% (+ 5% per 100 bonus AD) of target's maximum health bonus physical damage to the target. You then deal 20% increased damage to the target for the next 3 seconds (25 second cooldown; 500 range).

Regicide

Unique - End the Line: At the start of the combat phase in each round, declare the enemy champion with the lowest total maximum health as the Regent. Scoring a takedown against the Regent for the first time in the combat phase grants you 10 bonus attack damage permanently and sets the current cooldown of your ultimate ability to 0.5 seconds.

Flesheater

Unique - Hack the Meat: Dealing damage reduces the target's armor and magic resistance by 3 for 5 seconds, stacking up to 10 times for a total resistances reduction of 30. Stacks can be applied to each enemy only once every second.

Unique - Cannibalize: If you or the allied champion on your team score a takedown against an enemy champion, you are both healed for 18% of the taken down target's maximum health.

Hemomancer's Helm

Unique - Scarlet Allegiance: If your life steal and omnivamp sum to at least 30%, gain 500 bonus health and heal yourself equal to 10% of the damage that enemy champions in a range 525 units radius around you take from all sources.

Kayn's Best Augments

A few of the augments I like to use if I find them and what they do.

Best Augment for Each Rarity

Earthwake

Dashing or blinking causes you to leave behind a trail from the location you moved from that detonates after 0.75 seconds in a 300 width radius, dealing up to 80 - 2000 (based on level) (+ 100% bonus AD) (+ 30% AP) physical damage to enemies hit.

Bread and Butter

Your champion's first basic ability (Q) gains 200 ability haste.

Spin to Win

Your spinning abilities deal 30% increased damage and benefit from 30 ability haste.

Earthwake is great especially in early game before you get your form as it adds a lot of damage to your kit. Bread and Butter greatly reduces your Q cooldown, which lets you spam it in fights and deal a ton of AOE damage. Spin to Win is one of the best silver augments you can get since it's a free damage boost.

Other than these augments, you'll typically want to choose augments that give you a lot of extra damage, cooldown reduction, or speed.
